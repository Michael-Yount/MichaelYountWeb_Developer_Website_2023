Kitchen Prep App

This is an ongoing project. This application is designed to boost team productivity in commercial kitchens. It helps to keep kitchen teams informed about what is currently happening in the kitchen, from ordering to food cost analysis. Features will include: Company recipe book and searchable recipes on the web, measurments and conversions for recipes, food safety regulations and quick reference, company events and scheduling.
